The Arthouse Hotel

Featuring original architecture dating back to 1836 when it served as The School of Arts, this distinctive building has continuously evolved amid the city's ever-changing fortunes.

Accommodation

The venue for the wedding is right in the heart of Sydney's CBD so you really have a massive selection of hotels, apartments and even B&B's. Here are some hotels that we can recommend but of course feel free to book wherever suits!

QT Hotel This is where we are staying!

The Hilton Right next door to The Arthouse

Hyde Park Inn Short walk to the venue across from Hyde Park

Swissotel Across from QT Hotel next to Pitt St Mall

Sydney City Lodge Rennovated terrace in the city, nothing fancy but cheap and in a great location

Meriton Serviced Apartments Great chain of serviced apartments. There are several in the CBD

Hotel Coronation Cheap but nice rooms above a pub. Just around the corner from The Arthouse

Travelodge Sydney About 1km from The Arthouse, cheap and clean and in a handy location
